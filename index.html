Get to know me!

Hey! It's Anton Musalov and I'm a Mechatronics Engineer located in Canterbury, NZ. I've built an automated product testing station using an embedded QR code system, developed a 3-axis spraying robot for the USA market, and created a high accuracy cow detection algorithm using SICK IR sensors. I'm also skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, SASS, GIT, Shopify, Wordpress, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Android, and iOS.

I'm a bit of a digital product junky. Over the years, I've used hundreds of web and mobile apps in different industries and verticals. Feel free to contact me here.
