Get to know me!

Hey! I'm Anton Musalov, a passionate Mechatronics Engineer based in Canterbury, NZ. With a firm belief in the potential of automation, I've been instrumental in various projects, delivering innovative and cost-effective solutions. My ability to communicate effectively, combined with a consistently positive approach, makes me a valuable team member and a pleasure to collaborate with. Outside of work, I enjoy team sports and love to embrace the outdoors.

I specialise in robotic system designs, seamlessly integrating mechanical, electrical, and software elements. I boast expertise in Solidworks for weldment and sheetmetal design, and I'm well-versed in industrial electrical design and safety protocols. My software skills span from Python and C++ to ST.

Throughout my career, I've tackled diverse software, electrical, and mechanical challenges. As I continue to forge a path in automation and robotics, I welcome opportunities to connect and collaborate. Please feel free to contact me.
