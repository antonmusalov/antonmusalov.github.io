Product Testing Station

During summer internship of 2021 I designed and built a product testing station to automate the production process.

Project Summary

In a quest to overcome the challenges posed by manual and non-standardized product testing procedures, this project enabled the development of an automated solution. It involved devising a stationary jig setup equipped with servo motors to activate infrared sensors on the stepovers. This initiative honed my skills in system automation, problem-solving, and standardization of procedures.

The introduction of a microcontroller facilitated monitoring of the spray sequence and allowed for operator-based visual inspections, enhancing my understanding of system integration and control processes. The implementation of a Human-Machine Interface (HMI) for inputting assembly numbers and testing outcomes helped refine my skills in creating user-friendly digital interfaces.

I gained experience in system setup and operation, along with a comprehensive understanding of data logging protocols. The project further emphasized the importance of network connectivity, strengthening my skills in wireless and wired network systems. The project required high attention to waterproofing due to the harsh conditions in the workshop.
